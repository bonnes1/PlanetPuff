Game concept

A competitive co-op arcade party game that is meant to be played with friends. Players control running characters on a spherical world, all players leave a trail behind them, if a player intersects a trail they lose.

Game Play & features

Experience the intense game of Puff planet and compete with your friends. You can use an Xbox, PS controller or keyboard, it does not matter. There are a lot of strategies to discover and use to win the game. Moreover, You will be able to use a speed boost to tent traps for the other players. A perfect network connection that allows you to play without any interruption and totally smoothness. Furthermore, some basic mechanics make the game a delight. Finally, the cartoon and cute art aspect create a feeling of happiness in our players.
